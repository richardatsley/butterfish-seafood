At Butterfish Seafood we love to rock the boat! Not in a metaphorical sense! One of our employees jobs is to run from each side of the boat to the other and jump to make as much sway as possible for our guests! Our plan is to charge as much as possible for each meal so your heart really starts pounding when you have to throw it all up over the edge into the great blue!

Co-Founder of Butterfish, Emily Kelp is an invaluable member of our team. She made a new fish! We have asked many times how she did it, there are no previous records of the fish existing in any database of any kind. Whenever we ask she just looks away and starts a new conversation.

Our head Chef, David Oceanpun LOVES to cook. There's nothing like, special about him though, we don't have some surreal bit to add to the bottom of his image. He smokes cigarettes sometimes, but it's a boat, not a five star kitchen, he's an adult he can do whatever he wants.
